About Shrine LifeScience Pvt. Ltd.


We are manufacturer of pure quality of Nutraceuticals and Dietary Supplements. M/s Shrine Healthcare Pvt Ltd is manufacturer of Nutraceuticals and Dietary Supplements based in Gandhinagar, India, we have one of the most sophisticated and contemporary manufacturing facilities with the best plant equipment available today.

For the manufacturing and quality control of our premium products, we adhere to all WHO_GMP plans. Our expertise is in the development and production of premium nutraceuticals and dietary supplements for a variety of health conditions, including those related to the heart, diabetes, weight loss, women's health, bone and joint care, general nutrition, and stress reduction. We acquire our raw materials for our products from manufacturers throughout the world and only utilise the highest quality nutraceuticals ingredients. Rendering the applications that our valued clients request, we design and manufacture the products. All products and labels are created to fulfil with the legal requirements of the countries where they will be used. Our products are considered to supplement the user's diet with the most effective amounts of active nutraceuticals possible.

We are expert at creating and producing top nutraceuticals and dietary supplements on contract and under private label for all interested businesses worldwide. Our manufacturing measures are designed to reduce the loss of micronutrients and active phytochemicals. We create the product's delivery mechanism so that the products maintain their label claim over their whole shelf life, maximise the absorption of nutrients and phytochemicals, and are simple for customers to use.

For the local Indian market, we have created a whole line of nutraceuticals and dietary supplements. All of the items have been created with Indian consumers in mind. The items are created and packaged with the Indian environment in mind. Each product complies with Indian dietary laws. Our goal is to improve Indians' quality of life by giving them superior nutritional supplements and dietary foods that will keep them fit, healthy, and disease-free.
